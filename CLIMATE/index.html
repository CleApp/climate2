What Is Climate Change❓

It’s our planet’s long-term mood swing—temperatures and weather shifting over decades or centuries.

  • 👩‍🔬 Natural shifts happen (volcanoes 🌋, solar cycles ☀️), but since the 1800s…
  • 🏭 Humans are the main cause—burning coal, oil & gas releases greenhouse gases.
  • 🧣 Greenhouse gases (CO₂ & CH₄) wrap Earth in a heat-trapping blanket.
  • 🚗 Driving, 🔥 heating, 🌳 deforestation, and 🌾 farming all add to the problem.
  • ⚡ Energy, industry, transport, buildings, agriculture & land use are big emitters.
